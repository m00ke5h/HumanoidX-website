About Us

At HumanoidXVITCC, we aim to build and integrate innovative and new technologies in the ever growing field of Humanoid Robots.

The club enables one to learn, not only technical skills, but also life skills such as time management and team building, allowing holistic growth.

We believe that our future will be greatly defined by AI and automation and we, the HumanoidXVITCC aims to enhance one's overall skills in a manner that with the upcoming advancements in future, they will not only be able to cope up with it but also provide valuable inputs to the technology.
